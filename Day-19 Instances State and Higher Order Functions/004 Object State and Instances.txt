🔍 What’s Happening Here?
🐢 1. Ek Turtle Banana Aata Hai, Par Bahut Saari Kaise Banayenge?
Humne pehle ek turtle (Timmy) banaya tha:

import turtle
timmy = turtle.Turtle()  # Yeh ek turtle object hai!

Lekin agar race karni hai, to sirf ek turtle se kaam nahi chalega!
Hume multiple turtles chahiye… Tommy, Jenny, Benny, Johnny! 🎉
Sabko ek race me daalna hai!


🏗️ 2. Classes and Objects – Masti Bhari Duniya!
Samajho ki Turtle ek class hai – matlab blueprint.
Aur jo bhi turtles hum banayenge, woh sab is blueprint ke example (instance) honge.
Matlab:

Timmy ek turtle hai

Tommy bhi ek turtle hai

Dono independent hai, apna kaam kar sakte hain!

Jaise tum aur tumhare dost sab insaan (humans) ho, lekin sabki alag-alag identity hai! 😎


🎨 3. Har Turtle Ki Alag Pehchaan! (Attributes & State)
Agar Timmy aur Tommy same hote, to race boring hoti na?
Isliye, har turtle alag color, alag speed aur alag movement rakh sakti hai!

Jaise:

timmy.color("green")  # Timmy hara ho gaya!
tommy.color("purple")  # Tommy jamuni (purple) ho gaya!

Agar Timmy aage badh raha hai aur Tommy chup chap khada hai, to ye bhi alag state hai!

🎯 Final Takeaway – Tum Kya Seekhe?
1️⃣ Turtle ek class hai, jo blueprint ki tarah kaam karti hai.
2️⃣ Ek class se multiple objects ban sakte hain (Timmy, Tommy, Jenny…).
3️⃣ Har object ka apna alag color, speed aur movement ho sakta hai!
